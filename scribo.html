ⓘ About

Scribo

A handwritten conversation with and made with AI — draw, ask, and get replies with live LaTeX, across as many pages as you like. I did not like how I interacted with AI. I still like doing my calculations and get help. This is what I came up with. Works with Claude, Gemini, or local models via Ollama.

🔒 Your privacy

Scribo has no server and collects nothing. Your API keys and settings are saved only on this device, in your browser's local storage (not cookies — they're never sent anywhere). Your boards stay in the page while you work; nothing is uploaded unless you export it.

When you press Ask, only that request goes directly to the AI provider you chose (Anthropic, Google, or your own local Ollama). Clearing your browser data — or the app's storage — erases your saved keys and settings.

❔ How to use Scribo

✍️ Writing & drawing

Pick an ink colour and thickness, then draw on the board with a stylus, finger, or mouse. The ⬜ erase tool rubs out strokes. The board grows downward as you fill it. In ⚙ Settings → Board appearance you can change the line spacing or hide the ruled / margin lines.

🆎 Typed text & equations

Click 🆎 text, then click where you want text. A floating bar lets you choose font, size and colour. Tick parse LaTeX to render maths — type $E=mc^2$ for inline or $$…$$ for a centred equation. Double-click a LaTeX text block later to edit it.

🖼 Images

Click 🖼 image to pick a file, or just drag-and-drop / paste an image onto the board. Click an image to select it, drag to move, pull the blue corner to resize, or hit ✕ / Delete to remove.

✉ Asking the AI

Click 🔲 select, then choose ▭ box or ◯ lasso mode. In box mode, click two corners to set the area. In lasso mode, draw a freehand loop around what you want. Then the bar lets you ✉ Ask the AI about just that region, ⧉ copy it (then double-tap anywhere to paste it as a movable image), ✥ move it (contents and AI text travel together), ⤡ resize to scale the contents bigger/smaller (box only — drag the corner), or ⌫ erase everything inside. The 💬 chat panel is for typed back-and-forth. Use the 📎 button there to attach files (PDFs, images, text/code/data) as context for the AI's reply — PDFs are read natively by Claude; other models read images and text files.

📑 PDF annotation

📑 PDF loads a PDF onto the board (all pages stacked) to draw on and export. 📖 read PDF opens the paged reader instead: a large document opens page-by-page in a pane beside your board, so even huge files stay light. Use ‹ › or the page box to navigate, ＋ − to zoom, draw right on the page (annotations are kept per page as you flip), 🔲 select a region and ✉ Ask the AI about it, and ⬇ page to save the current page with your annotations as an image.

📚 Citing sources

In ⚙ Settings, turn on 📚 Cite sources for technical results and the AI will name theorems/results and point to references (original papers, Wikipedia, nLab, etc.). References come from the model's training, so verify them — they're leads, not citations of record.

↶ Undo

The ↶ undo button (or Cmd/Ctrl+Z) reverses your recent board actions — strokes, shapes, pastes, erases, typed text, and AI replies — up to 25 steps back. Undo history is per page and resets when you switch pages.

📐 Shapes

Click 📐 shapes and pick a shape (line, arrow, rectangle, ellipse, triangle) from the dropdown beside it. Choose a line style (solid, dashed, dotted) and set the line width with the slider. Tap once to set the start point, then tap again for the end point — or drag in one motion. Hold Shift for even proportions (square, circle, equilateral). Switch back to a pen colour to free-draw again.

📄 Pages

Each tab is a separate page with its own drawing and its own conversation. Use ＋ page / 🗑 page to add or remove them.

🤖 Choosing the AI

The toolbar dropdown switches model on the fly. In ⚙ Settings you choose a provider — Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, or Local (Ollama) — and add one or more API keys per provider. If a key hits a limit, Scribo auto-switches to your next key for that provider. The Max reply length slider controls how long answers can be. Turn on 🔍 Verify answers to have a second model (your choice) independently check each answer — its verdict appears in a green 🔍 block below the reply.

🖥 Local models (free, private)

Install Ollama, run ollama pull qwen2.5vl:7b, then choose Local (Ollama) in Settings. Nothing leaves your computer. If connection fails, start it with OLLAMA_ORIGINS=* ollama serve.

💾 Saving

Save project (.scribo) stores everything — all pages, drawings, text, images and AI chats — so you can reopen it later with Open project and keep working. Scribo also auto-saves to your browser as you go, and offers to restore your last session when you return. For sharing or printing: PNG = flat image; PDF = print-ready with rendered equations (wait for “Ready ✓”, then print); HTML = re-openable view-only snapshot with live maths.

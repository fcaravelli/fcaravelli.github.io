Hello, I'm Francesco Caravelli

I am a theoretical physicist. I am currently the head of the Theory and Modeling Group at Planckian, a spin-off of Scuola Normale Superiore and the University of Pisa (where I am also a guest). Previously, I was a Staff Scientist at Los Alamos National Laboratory, where I am still a guest scientist. I work in quantum and neuromorphic computing, complex systems, network theory, and statistical physics.

My research focuses on the intersection of physics and computation — exploring how structure and dynamics interact across scales.

  • Evolving Networks with Memory
    MATLAB package for simulating networks with reinforcing walkers, developed from the research published in EPL 109, 28006 (2015). This software models evolving and growing networks subject to reinforcement dynamics and decay, with support for multiple types of dynamics, directed or undirected graphs, weighted links, and control over initial graph structure. The simulator can analyze clustering, thermalization times, and graph observables, and can also generate animations such as this example video.

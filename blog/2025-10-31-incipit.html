Incipit

October 31, 2025

Welcome to my new website — a modern, open, and extensible space to share my research, projects, and occasional thoughts. After several years of maintaining a simple Google Site, I decided to migrate to GitHub Pages to create something lighter, more flexible, and long-lived.

The structure of this new site reflects my research trajectory — from quantum thermodynamics and neuromorphic computing to complex systems and network theory. Each section of the website evolves independently, and I intend to make it a space where updates, publications, and open-source codes naturally intersect.

This first post is, in a sense, an “incipit” — a beginning. I’ll use this blog to document ongoing ideas, collaborations, and insights that bridge physics and computation.

Future entries may include commentary on new publications, open-source software updates, and reflections on topics like energy landscapes, memristive systems, and quantum computation. Expect some equations like $E = mc^2$ or $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{J} = 0$ to appear along the way.

Thank you for visiting — and welcome to this new digital notebook.
